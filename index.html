About the job

We are looking for a Senior Product Manager to own our payments infrastructure roadmap — from checkout optimisation to settlement flows.


What you will do:

  • Define and drive the product roadmap for Razorpay's core payments platform
  • Work with engineering and design to reduce payment failure rates for 8M+ merchants
  • Own checkout conversion metrics and drive improvements across the funnel
  • Collaborate with business and partnerships teams on new payment method integrations

What we are looking for:

  • 5+ years of product management experience, preferably in fintech or payments
  • Strong analytical mindset — comfortable with SQL, dashboards, and data-driven decisions
  • Experience working on platform or infrastructure products
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
